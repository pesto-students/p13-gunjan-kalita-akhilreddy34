
What are the primary keys and foreign keys in a relational database, and how do they establish relationships between tables?

PRIMARY KEYS: Primary key is a key which uniquely identifies a row ina table or we can say primary key is one of the candidate key.
FOREIGN KEY: foreign key is a key which establishes the relationship between two tables they establish the relationship by inserting  and referencing the primary key of one table as foreign key to other table.
